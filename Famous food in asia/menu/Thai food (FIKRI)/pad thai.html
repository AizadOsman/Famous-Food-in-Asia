No. 4 - Famous Food in Thailand

Pad Thai

Back to Famous Food In Thailand

Name Pad Thai, is a stir-fried rice noodle dish
Taste With the slippery noodles, signature sweet-savoury flavour, sprinkle of peanuts and tang from lime, this is a Thai food favourite for good reason.
Ingredients Pad thai is made with rehydrated dried rice noodles with some tapioca flour mixed in, which are stir fried with eggs and chopped firm tofu, flavored with tamarind juice, fish sauce, dried shrimp, garlic or shallots, red chili pepper and palm sugar, and served with lime wedges and often chopped roasted peanuts.It may contain other vegetables like bean sprouts, garlic chives, pickled radishes or turnips, and raw banana flowers. It may also contain fresh shrimp, crab, squid, chicken or other fish or meat.
In Popular Culture Pad thai is listed at number five on a list of "World's 50 most delicious foods" readers' poll compiled by CNN Go in 2011.
The Thai film Jao saao Pad Thai uses pad thai as a plot device as the heroine claims she will marry whoever eats her pad thai for 100 days in a row.
