Gaeng Kiow Wan Kai

Back to Famous Food In Thailand

Name Gaeng Keow Wan Gai (Thai Chicken Green Curry)
Taste Delicious creamy soup dish with an abundance of fresh vegetables, roots, spices and tender chicken cooked in a tasteful blend of green curry paste and coconut cream.
Ingredients Green curry paste chicken, chilies, Thai eggplant, bamboo shoots, Thai basil, coconut milk, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, cumin, fish sauce, soy sauce, oil, sugar, coriander, lime juice,
Serving Green curry is typically eaten with rice as part of a wider range of dishes in a meal, or with round rice noodles known as khanom chin as a single dish.
